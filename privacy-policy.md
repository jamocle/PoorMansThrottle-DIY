# Privacy Policy
## Poor Man’s Throttle

Last updated: 2026-03-14

Poor Man’s Throttle is a mobile application designed for model railroad hobbyists to wirelessly control locomotives using compatible hardware.

Your privacy is important. This application is designed to operate locally and does not collect or share personal information.

---

# Information Collection

Poor Man’s Throttle does **not collect, store, or transmit personal information**.

The app:

- does not require user accounts
- does not collect personal data
- does not track users
- does not use analytics services
- does not display advertising
- does not share information with third parties

---

# Bluetooth and Wi-Fi Communication

Poor Man’s Throttle communicates with compatible external hardware using **Bluetooth and Wi-Fi**.

These wireless connections are used solely to send control commands to supported throttle hardware devices.

The app:

- does not collect personal data during Bluetooth or Wi-Fi communication
- does not transmit personal information
- does not send information to external servers

All communication occurs directly between the mobile device and the compatible hardware.

---

# Data Storage

Poor Man’s Throttle does not store personal information.

Any configuration settings are stored locally on the user's device and are not transmitted outside the device.

---

# Third-Party Services

Poor Man’s Throttle does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

---

# Children's Privacy

Poor Man’s Throttle does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Because the application does not collect personal data, it is safe for users of all ages.
