COLORS in PMT

Make PMT look like your railroad.

PMT now includes theming, so you can personalize the app with colors that match your railroad, your club, or just your own sense of style. Keep it classic, go bold, or build a color palette that turns heads the next time someone sees your throttle.

Theming in Settings and Pick Color

The new theming capability lets you customize the look of PMT throughout the interface. From the Settings screen, you can adjust brand colors used across windows, buttons, text, and other interface elements. When you tap a color picker, the Pick Color screen gives you direct control over the exact look you want.

This is more than a cosmetic tweak. It gives PMT personality. You can mirror the colors of your railroad, coordinate with a favorite locomotive scheme, match a club identity, or create something playful and unmistakably yours.

1
Open Settings Go to Settings and look under the Appearance section to see the themeable color entries available across the app.
2
Tap Pick Choose any item you want to personalize, then tap Pick to open the Pick Color screen.
3
Make it yours Adjust the color, preview the change, and apply it to start building a PMT theme that looks custom and deliberate.
Match your railroad Build a theme around your railroad’s official colors and give PMT a look that feels connected to the equipment you run.
Show off a little A custom theme is a fun way to make your setup stand out and, yes, impress friends the next time they see it in action.
Keep experimenting Try bold accent colors, clean modern combinations, or high-contrast themes that make PMT feel fresh every time you open it.
PMT theming is meant to be enjoyable. It gives the app a more personal feel and turns everyday control into something that looks polished, custom, and a little more fun.
Note Sometimes a restart of the app is required, or the screen may need to be redisplayed before a color change becomes visible. Some devices do not instantly show display updates.
